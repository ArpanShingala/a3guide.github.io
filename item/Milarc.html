Milarc

Increases Attack as well as Attack attributes for specific time

Buy Price: 300,000

It is a Gold tinted Glistening Liquid which becomes transparent when in contact with light. It is made by combining various Larcs and some Mysterious Golden Powder
Increases Basic attack by 150 and Elemental Attack by 50 for 4 minutes.
- Multiple Milarcs cannot be used at once
- Using Milarc when a Larc is active will dispel the attributes provided by the Larc
- Using a Larc when Milarc is active will dispel the attributes provided by the Milarc
