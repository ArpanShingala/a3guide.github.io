Epithymia

Archer Bow

Grade 9

Sell Price: 22,200,000

There was a Evil King who wanted to be the most powerful human ever, so he worshipped demons. He was bestowed this Bow for his prayers, but it had some conditions which stated that he must kill people in order to extract their soul and get power in return. Epithymia in Language of Hellmarsh means " The Ruthless Killer"
