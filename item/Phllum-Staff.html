Phllum Staff

Elemental Mage Staff

Grade 2

Buy Price: 99,800

Puleinyl helped Phllum of the spirit tribe to be born of rainwater. They are optimistic spirits who enjoy singing and dancing, but they can be crueler than anybody else when ordered by Puleinyl. So they have a bad reputation among the other tribes. As they are dexterous, they sometimes make unusual presents and give them to humans they like. Legend runs that they gave this staff to a mage who visited Heyzend. Anyone who uses this staff will be able to enlist the holy powers of the cloud and rain.
