Kwaon Sword

Warrior Sword

Grade 6

Buy Price: 8,982,000

This famous sword used to be owned by the legendary knight, Kwaon. In search of his missing friends, Kwaon was taken by an irresistible force to a place where there was a golden tree, and this sword was floating in the hollow inside the tree. Legend says that no shield could withstand Kwaon smashing the Kwaon sword against it. 95~105cm long, and 15~20cm thick, the sword for cutting does not have any hand protector with the blade going straight up from the hilt. The end of the hilt is bent like a horn. It is the heaviest sword of all.
