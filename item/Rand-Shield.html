Rand Shield

Mace Holy Knight Shield

Grade 4

Buy Price: 261,600

Leather is placed on a wooden plate, and a red bronze plate with a coat of arms inscribed is added to it with rivets. In the middle the insignia of the Rand family is inscribed. The Rand family is one of the distinguished families of Agonyl, and is very influential as Tole has absolute trust in the family. This shield is used by the paladin who belong to and serve the Rand family.
