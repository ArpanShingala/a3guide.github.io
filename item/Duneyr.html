Duneyr

Holy Knight Mace

Grade 7

Buy Price: 29,940,000

As its end is pointed, it can inflict deeper wounds. Characteristically, it has half-moon-shaped protrusions on the handle. It was designed this way to enable the knight using this mace to injure his enemies with the handle. It is said to have been created by a saint warrior during the war with gods. As it weighs more than it seems, it is difficult to handle.
