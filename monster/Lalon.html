Lalon

Level: 115
Health: 4000
Defense: 50
Property: Ice

Attack: 600~2900
Repawn Time: 1 minutes

He is creature who is born from a curse. He is a poison producing creature with broken nails and Corroded Yellow Teeth. His special attributes are his longer skull, Giant Hands and Feet. He deals tremendous amounts of damage but his defense is weak.
