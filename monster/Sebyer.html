Sebyer

Level: 145
Health: 31000
Defense: 425
Property: Fire

Attack: 750~1250
Repawn Time: 30 seconds

He was a archer who was trained by the army of Emperor Rubal, however he was frequently reminded of his dead family and attempted suicide. This attempt failed and the toxins corroded his skin away. He wanders Tocsique looking for Rubal.
