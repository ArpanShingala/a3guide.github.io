Skicaboreck

Level: 140
Health: 57000
Defense: 450
Property: Lightning

Attack: 800~1350
Repawn Time: 40 seconds

He was opposed to the gods as he always believed that gods are using humans like slaves. He was eventually exiled to Vellgrador for not obeying the gods. He stayed in Vellgrador Forest where he spent rest of his life protecting it against threats. After his death, the forest reincarnated him as Skicaboreck.
