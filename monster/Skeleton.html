Skeleton

Level: 148
Health: 92500
Defense: 500
Property: Fire

Attack: 800~1530
Repawn Time: 40 seconds

It is a skeleton wearing torn clothes and has a bow in its hand. Skeleton was once the most powerful archer in Paratopis, who was killed during the fight. It was reborn as a zombie. Now it hunts to find the people who turned Paratopis into a graveyard.
