Arr Phameino

Level: 135
Health: 300000
Defense: 600
Property: Fire

Attack: 900~1700
Repawn Time: 2 hours (Variable)

One arm warpped in molten lava and the other arm strong as iron, Arr Phameino poses many threats to its opponents. There are myriad of arrows and spears stuck to his back. It has gained fame as the only creature who was able to stab Rabellu. He is head of the Phameino and controls Phameino and Di Phameino.
