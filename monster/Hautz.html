Hautz

Level: 100
Health: 5500
Defense: 240
Property: Lightning

Attack: 375~440
Repawn Time: 20 seconds

He was once the the Spirit of the Wind. He has strange anatomy where his shoulder, chest and head are joined together without the presence of a neck along with four transparent arms. He also dual wields two axes and with the help of the winds he can use them with blazing speeds.
