Kasthol

Level: 57
Health: 2500
Defense: 178
Property: Lightning

Attack: 260~300
Repawn Time: 20 seconds

He was once a very reputed knight of Quanato who tried to warn the king that they were traitors amongst their mist, but he did not pay heed to him which caused his death. Kasthol was blamed and killed for the king's death as he was the last one seen with the King. His hatred caused him to reincarnate and wanders to find the people who did this.
