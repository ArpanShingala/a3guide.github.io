Di Phameino

Level: 135
Health: 100000
Defense: 500
Property: Lightning

Attack: 700~1200
Repawn Time: 2 hours (Variable)

Di Phameino is a lesser creature compared to the Phameino. It is created by the cell shredded by Phameino. It is observed that they consider Phameino as their master and will follow any command issued by Him.
