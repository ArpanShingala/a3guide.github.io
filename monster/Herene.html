Herene

Level: 115
Health: 8200
Defense: 280
Property: Fire

Attack: 550~720
Repawn Time: 1 minutes

She is half serpent and half woman who is capable of using Ice based Magic. Her smile can fool even the most densest of warriors. She is known to have a liking to human meat and is often used to scare children to sleep. In desperate situations she can eat her own species.
