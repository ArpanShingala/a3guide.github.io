Keteal

Level: 148
Health: 97500
Defense: 500
Property: Lightning

Attack: 700~1500
Repawn Time: 40 seconds

Keateal's sturdy sheep has four sharp canines in front of her chin, and a single isosceles triangle tooth in her upper and lower jaws. There is also a long dorsal fin, similar to a winged wing. Keateal was an ancient animal of the water snake that lived in Sirpel past. However, after the Iron Age, she grew her powers by eating the rotting sea creatures and as a result she became a monster in Sirpel.
