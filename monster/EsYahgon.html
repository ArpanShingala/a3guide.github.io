EsYahgon

Level: 115
Health: 12000
Defense: 230
Property: Ice

Attack: 500~800
Repawn Time: 3 minutes

It is the leader of the Fourth Floor of Rangdel.It is a odd looking creature which wears rusted armor only on the left side of its body, it also has spiked and crooked teeth. It prefers punching its enemy and its body is covered by mucus which can disable its opponent or help it keep warmth in the cold of Rangdel. He is also sluggishly slow.
