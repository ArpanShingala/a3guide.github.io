Statue Kartere

Level: 140
Health: 400000
Defense: 800
Property: Lightning

Attack: 1500~1900
Repawn Time: 2 hours 48 minutes

This creature/statue should not be taken lightly as it has the power to destroy a creature before the blink of an eye. This creature is Leader of Fifth Floor of Randel which is Inaccessible by Teleporters and is the master of the most powerful spells.
