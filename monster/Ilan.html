Level: 148
Health: 87500
Defense: 500
Property: Ice

Attack: 750~1580

Ilan hated the recklessness of the humans who waged war with God because the spiritual disturbances caused her appearence to change and as revenge she deceived humans by her beautiful singing and eventually killed them.
