System Requirements

Before Starting the game, we need to check the specifications of our PC. The following are the minimum and recommended specifications of a PC that is compatible to play A3


Minimum Requirements Recommended Requirements
Pentium 4 Pentium 4, 2.4 Ghz and Above
512 MB RAM 1 GB RAM and Above
NVIDIA GeForce 3 Series NVIDIA GeForce 6 Series/ ATI Radeon HD 4000 series and Above
Microsoft Windows 98 Windows 7 or higher

We recommend gamers to use the recommended specifications for best graphic effects and improved quality of game. Please download the latest drivers of your Graphics card from below:

Nvidia Series

ATI Radeon Series

Please download the latest Direct X for improved results and smooth functioning of the Game. We recommend to use Direct X 9.0c or later, which can be downloaded from below:

Microsoft Direct X
