Warrior

Strength: 30
Dexterity: 16
HP: 30
MP: 20

Sex: Male
Age: 26

Over 190cm tall, he has a very well developed upper body with muscles, sharp brown eyes & hair down to his shoulders, reticent lips and well tanned skin, all showing off his naturally born physique as a Warrior. He provokes his enemy with an imposing, dignified attitude. Status points are used to upgrade Str., Dex., HP, MP. As Master of close encounter Combat, Warriors use two hand Swords, Spears, and Axes as their main weapons. They can't equip shields for protection. HP & MP status automatically rise at Level Up.
