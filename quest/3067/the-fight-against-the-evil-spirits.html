The fight against the evil spirits
Dreng has finally come up with the last quest, this time I have to finish some evil spirits. It is said that the Semeusos of Paratopis have transformed hunters into evil zombies called skeletons. These skeletons have disturbed the natural balance in Heyzand and are to be taught a good lesson. Let's hunt 30 skeletons and return to Dreng for a promising reward.
