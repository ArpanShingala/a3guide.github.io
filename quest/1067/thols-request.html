But Thol needs 2 more Hearts of Ellma and 2 Hearts of Lalon. He has promised twice the reward for bringing the hearts to him. I'll have to hunt at least 25 of each to obtain their hearts... he says he's got some business to attend to and cannot come along. Let's get this over with!
