Grnita Heath
This was a secret labyrinth, made to protect cities and allow refugees to travel faster to refugee zones. However even the builders have no idea how to traverse the maze. Hence no one has returned once entering it. It also contains the Graybone's Room, which containg Graybone, the most powerful creature Egonihl has seen.
