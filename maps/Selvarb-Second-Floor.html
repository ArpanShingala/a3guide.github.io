Selvarb Second Floor
This floor is identical to Ground Floor, once entered to this floor there no returning back. The Crytal tree is still in the center providing a way to mark the direction heading to prevent getting lost. The only way to advance is to enter the Third Floor whose entrance is on the opposite side of the floor.
