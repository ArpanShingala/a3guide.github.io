Clororence
It has a cool season and warm climate. It is regarded by most as a land of paradise as all the resouces like coal, grains, Juniper, fragrant wood etc. are present in abundant amounts and can produce anything which is required by its residents. There is a lot of water that it overflows into farms, but due to its magical properties it produces fruits rather than wilting. The trees here are said to be low lying which allows people to easily get the fruits.
