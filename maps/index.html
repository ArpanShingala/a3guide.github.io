Egonihl
The story takes place in a place called Hellmarsh which is located in south of a continent called Egonihl. Egonihl is covered by all sides with seas of lava and water making transportaion from and to Egonihl impossible without help of mages. Hellmarsh is a poor country which came widely known as the "Hometown of Warriors". Hellmarsh got its name for its efforts against the Rogue God Rabellu. Hellmarsh has many mountains ranges one of which is Rabat which spans from Temoz and ends at Quanato. North of Hellmarsh contains regions with Swamps and a Tropical Climates like Vermeden, Niflgarr and Fairmode which the Southern part is a wasteland.
