Item Options

What are Item Options

Items like Weapons, Armors and Shields have the ability to have additional options which provide benfit to the wearer. These options are sometimes present by default and sometimes are required to be added manually through several procedures.

Item options can be added by using Item Combinations or Item Gem Slotting

Default Item Options

Any weapons or armors which are created with combinations will not bear any options. They will be Level 1 only. Items acquired through hunting carry random options and can be revealed after Identification.

Basic Options

Basic options consist of the following.

Blessing

Having the blessing option on the Items increases the success rate of Combinations and Item Gem Slotting associated with the item.

Item Mounting requirement reduced

Having this item will benefit you by decreasing the Strength, Dexterity and Intelligence requirement for wearing the item. This option can be 10%, 20% or 30%.

Additional Attack / Additional Defense

Additional Attack and Additional Defense can be added to the Weapons, Armors and Shields. The value of the added attack or defense is fixed and depends on the grade of the item.

Additional Attack can be added to Weapons only. Additional Defense can be added to all Armors and Shields.

Helmet Options

MP Absorption

When being attacked by monsters, MP Absorption provides additional MP for each attack dealt.

HP Absorption

When being attacked by monsters, HP Absorption provides additional HP for each attack dealt.

HP/MP Absorption for Skills

Reduces the HP/MP utilization for skills.

Armor Options

Defense (Ice)

Increases Ice elemental defense of the player.

Defense (Fire)

Increases Fire elemental defense of the player.

Defense (Lightning)

Increases Lightning elemental defense of the player.

Pants Options

Raise Accuracy

Increases the Accuracy of an attack.

Raise Evasion

Increases the chance to dodge an attack.

Raise Magic Evasion

Increases the chance to dodge spells.

Boots Options

Raise Critical Hit Evasion

Increases the chance to dodge Critical Hits.

Raise WZ Acquisition

Increases the Woonz Gained when killing monsters.

Raise Critical Hit Rate

Increases the chance of a Critical Hit.

Gloves Options

Increase Skill Duration

Increases the duration of lasting skills. Not applicable to Warrior gloves.

Raise Basic Attack Damage

Increases the Attack Power of the player. Not applicable to Mage gloves.

Raise Skill Attack Damage

Increases the Attack of the spells of a player.
