Player Killing

What are Passives?

Passives are skills which can be used to enhance a character's combat skills such as Hit Points and Evasion. Passives are learned with the use of Passive Skill Point(PSP) which are earned every level after Level 101.

  • Passives dont have to activated.
  • You can only learn Passive Skills once you are above Level 101.
  • 1 PSP is earned when a character above Level 101 levels up.
  • Passives Skills are not castable and hence provide status effects retroactively.
  • Passives always have a Positive and a Negative Effect. For Example : When Robust is skilled, your HP increases while your MP decreases.
  • All passives are permanent, unless they are resetted.
  • Passives can be stacked with other skills granting status effects.

How to Skill Passives?

To Skill Passive Skills, you will have to go to Hatrel and find the NPC Rameda interacting with him will open a menu with all the passive skills listed, along with their Positive and Negative Effects.

Clicking on a skill will prompt a menu confirming whether you want to skill the passive.



Bonus Passive Skill Points

After the player reaches 160 the next 5 levels will grant bonus Passive Skill Points.

How to unskill Passives?

When interacting with Rameda, a button labelled Reset can be noticed at the Bottom Right corner of his menu. Clicking that button will prompt a message comfirming the reset of Passive Skills. Resetting will unlearn all the learnt passives and resetting costs 100,000 Lore.
