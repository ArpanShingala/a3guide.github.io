Wolfreck Set
Using the traditional method of armor making by the Wolfreck family, this armor is as comfortable as a second layer of skin. Although the whole of Wolfreck family was destroyed in the war, the lucky escape of one of the blacksmiths that helped in making these armors managed to keep this type of armor in production.
