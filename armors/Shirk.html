Shirk Set
In the year 3442, Shirk, the knighthood leader of the first knighthood of Quanato was executed, bearing the title of a rebel to the Thol of Quanato. The first knighthood of Quanato assisted the Thol in establishing the country, and the increasing power that Shirk gained in the process made the Thol uneasy, which eventually led to his unjustly death. The Thol's second son, who succeeded his father as the Thol, did an investigation and cleared Shirk's name. Shirk's wife kept a part of her husband's armor secretly before she was exiled. Years later, her daughter reconstructed and restored the armor to its former glory, in the name of her father, the loyal subject who was consumed by the former Thol's greed for power.
