Exterr Set
This set is made from a metal called Geoniwa is very very hard to obtain, which is smelted in order to form powdered Exterr. This powdered exterr can also be obtained by a special plants but the time they take to bloom is lengthy and has a low change of give the powder. This armor became famous as Dun used it and he became near invincible.

Grade: 9
Class: Warrior
Set Type: Sword Warrior

Dexterity: 401
