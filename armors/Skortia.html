Skortia Set
It was said that a long time ago, a warrior challenged an evil demon. Although he couldn't defeat that demon, he managed to take its armor. He took the armor, left it in a native village, and left without a word. Rumor has it that he does not belong to Hellmarsh. The natives melted the armor and made Skortia, the armor that gives astonishing protection to its owner. As the armor can re-size itself according to its wearer, it is also said to have a life of its own.
