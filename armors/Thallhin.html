Thallhin Set
Thallhin is a kind of a stimulant that paralyzes the central nervous system of humans, and means "the god of death" in the language of Hellmash. In the past, it was often used to make warriors run berserk and increase their striking power. Because of its virulence, it was prohibited. The Thallhin set was made on the basis of the tendency of Thallhin to solidify like threads. If your skin is exposed to Thallhin, your nerve will be paralyzed and you won't feel a thing. So despite serious wounds, you will never stop fighting. So troops wearing this set were feared by not only enemies but also friendly forces.
