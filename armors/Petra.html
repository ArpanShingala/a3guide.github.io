Petra Set
Petra Armor, with parts of red dazzling as if fire is dancing on it, is one of the most glamorous armor set. However, legend has it that numerous vengeful spirits possessed this armor, which led to the dazzling red aura that emits from it. The armor is red from the blood of those women who were brutally raped till death. In order to appease the spirits, the blacksmiths from the villages collected these blood red cloths to make this armor.

Grade: 10
Class: Archer
Set Type: Bow Archer

Level: 0
Strength: 445
