Ihenthal Set
The Ihenthal set boasts of outstanding strength and beauty. As sage Ekrki popularized Tizeruim, it is called the stone of a hermit. This Tizeruim is processed over a long period of time and made into the Ihenthal set, emitting the recondite color unique to Tizeruim which is good enough to make an evil-doing mage look like an angel. A paladin wanted to turn his sister, who was a mage at the time, into a paladin, and gave her a suit of armor as a present. The sister modified this armor to suit her occupation. This modified armor is the Ihenthal set, one of the most famous among those made of Tizeruim.
