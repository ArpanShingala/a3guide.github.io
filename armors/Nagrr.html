Nagrr Set
The Nagrr set was named after Naglfar, which means the ship of the dead. It is one of the defense gears made for the 11 Army Corps generals who distinguished themselves during the launch of the 45th evil punitive force. Unlike its name, it looks so sacred because it was designed with a little different purpose of guiding their friends killed in action to a better world. Sometimes paladins who try to look stylish use some chemicals to give it unlikely luster, but its original color is lusterless soft cream.
