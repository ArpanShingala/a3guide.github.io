Axesur Set
Blessed by the God of Light, this armor can bring the greatest potential out of any mage. Although it looks frail, this armor can complement its wearer by enhancing the magical damage dealt by the mage. However, it is said that the greatest advantage to having this armor is that it can demoralize the enemies. But due to the extreme difficulty in forging this armor, it is hardly available to most people.

Grade: 9
Class: Mage
Set Type: Elemental Mage

Set Requirements

Level: 130
Intelligence: 603
Dexterity: 204
Stat Requirement does not change with level of the set items
