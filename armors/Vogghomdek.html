Vogghomdek Set
The "golden knight" Vogghomdek once wore this armor. As this armor is made from small donations of gold collected over the years, it is extremely difficult to forge. However, looking at this magnificent set of armor, all kinds of hardships endured by any holy knight will seem worthwhile.

Grade: 10
Class: Holy Knight
Set Type: Sword Holy Knight
