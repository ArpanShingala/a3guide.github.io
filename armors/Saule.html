Saule Set
To commemorate the first lady Thol, who not only survived the harsh lands of Egonil, but also ruled and expanded Quanato for 20 years, this armor was made in her memory. Saule died in a battle while trying to save her soldiers from a demon. Touched by her actions, the whole country of Quanato joined in to make this armor for her funeral, hoping that it will escort her in her endless journey in the afterlife.

Grade: 8
Class: Mage
Set Type: Non Elemental Mage
