Dvalin Set
The family insignia of the Wirsha family, one of the most distinguished families of the Turohen tribe, is inscribed on this set. It is famous because it was blessed together with the Rguen shield by Mooter, the miraculous healer. It was mostly worn by the paladins hired by the Wirsha family. Dvalin, a famous paladin of the Wirsha family, made it all the more famous. It was originally named after the name of the blacksmith who made it, Rguen, but the fame of Dvalin rename it the Dvalin set.

Class: Holy Knight
