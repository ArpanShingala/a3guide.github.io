Blee Set
This is an armor set made by a loving husband for his wife in Hatrel, who swore revenge on the demons that killed her son. The wife went into battle with her bow and arrows, despite the desperate begging of her husband, telling her not to. It was not too long before he realized that the only thing he can do is to make the best armor he can for her. Therefore, this armor is named after his wife, Blee. It was said that whenever the wife is in danger, her husband's sword will react violently with certain unknown forces.

Class: Archer
Set Type: Bow Archer
