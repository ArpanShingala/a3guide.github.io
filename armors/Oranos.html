Oranos Set
Oranos Armor is made from Oranos, a special kind of mineral found in another continent. It is said that the maker of this armor, and how this armor is made, remains a mystery till this very day. Apart from providing the basic protection, this armor can bend in an amazing way, as if it is made of rattan. It is also as light as a feather, which makes it one of the better choices for most crossbow users.
