Ygg Set
The poison-spouting evil thing, Vobug (currently living in Rangdel) has strong poison throughout its body. So its intestines and blood were made into weapons and defense gears. When the atrocities of man-eating Vobug was known to people, a punitive force was sent, and the Ygg set was made out of the skin of Vobug they obtained at that time. The twisted skin filled with the dark red poison presents a striking contrast to the silver color. As the poison is too strong, a slight carelessness will endanger the lives of not only the hunters using this, but also those who make it. Perhaps because of this, only a very small number of people can make this set in the entire Hellmash region.
