Mordi Set
A long time ago, Graybone invaded Quanato and Temoz simultaneously. Graybone was a huge demon, brutal by nature, and can disintegrate the head of a person by 1/3 in a strike. This whole invasion was actually a plot from Rabellu, and because of this, the two countries went to war for as long as 6 years. Mordi armor set is the armor made from the bones of that demon.

Grade: 8
Class: Warrior
Set Type: Axe Warrior
