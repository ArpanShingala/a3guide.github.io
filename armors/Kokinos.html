Kokinos Set
Phorrea, the one who made the Phorrea Armor for her sister, made this Kokinos Armor at the age of 21. Her sister wore her Phorrea Armor and fought bravely, but died eventually. Full of remorse, Phorrea decided to make a new set of armor for her sister's best friend, who decided to revenge her sister's death. This armor is made from the skin of the salamander, which is extremely hardy.

Grade: 9
Class: Archer
Set Type: Bow Archer

Set Requirements

Level: 130
Strength: 415
Dexterity: 400
Stat Requirement does not change with level of the set items
