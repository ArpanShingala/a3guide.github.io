Tefron Set
Legend goes that a certain blacksmith made this defense gear in Whethert for his girlfriend. Only mages with magic powerful enough to enlist the power of darkness can wear it. If a less than capable mage wears it, it will decrease the magical power and physical strength of the wearer, resulting in his death.
