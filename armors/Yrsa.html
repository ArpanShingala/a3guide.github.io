Yrsa Set
Yrsa means 'a female wolf.' This famous defense gear was worn by the mage-knights under the command of Deneroo. It was named the Yrsa set because the strength of their magic and their majestic appearance resembled those of wolves. No one knew what it was originally made of and nobody could acquire it. But a blacksmith was fascinated by its destructive power and thereby reproduced it in the current form.

Grade: 7
Class: Mage
Set Type: Elemental Mage
