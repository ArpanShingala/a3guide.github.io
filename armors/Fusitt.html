Fusitt Set
Kardion is a metal found in the deep cave near the lava river, Lethebliver. As strong as to endure the high temperature and pressure of lava, it is recognized as the best material for making defense gears, but after Lavart became an active volcano, the mine sank below the lava river, stopping all production. It is as difficult to handle as it is strong, so few can refine this metal. The Fusitt set made by the family of Blacksmith Fusitt unrivaled in refining Kardion, is said to reflect the advantages and disadvantages of Kardion extremely well.
