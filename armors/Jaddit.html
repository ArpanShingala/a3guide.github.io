Jaddit Set
An evil spirit by the name of Jaddit is sealed in this armor. Those who are not strong enough will not even be able to get near it. Sadly, there are no detailed findings on this armor in the ancient text; it is only known that the armor was first discovered in the Silver Ages. How it was created remained a mystery. It is also said that if the wearer believes too much in his own strength and ability, he might be consumed by the spirit residing in the armor.
