Kwasir Set
It is more appropriate to call those Holy Knights that wear Kwasir Armor the Unholy Knights, as the evil aura that emits from this armor is overwhelming. A long time ago, an old man gave this armor to an evil knight. On the very first day of his battle, he died. This armor was then retrieved from the evil knight on his funeral by the very same old man that gave him this gift. From then on, everyone gave a nickname to this armor - The Armor of Judgment.
