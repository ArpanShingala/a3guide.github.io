Shllid Set
This defense gear is made of shllid, a metal filled with the energy of earth. This energy is said to help its wearer to get out of harm's way. Shllid is a metal that has been widely known since the golden age. Many stories told about it are often used as subject matters for troubadours. Particularly, the story about gods' persecution of those who gave a helping hand in the romance between the brave warrior, Wordirr, and Hey Terner. The shllid set has been almost out of circulation because Heyzend stopped supply of shllid after the expulsion of humans, but in recent years Jugonaha, the manager of a secret gambler's den in Vemerden, is secretly trading in it.
