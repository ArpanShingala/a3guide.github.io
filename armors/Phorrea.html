Phorrea Set
The basic materials for this armor set are cloth and leather, with some small metal protective pieces on the chest and gloves. It was said that the Phorrea Set was made by a little girl called Phorrea for her one and only sister. It's astonishing how a child can make such magnificent armor. Years later, Phorrea became the maker of some other armor sets too.
