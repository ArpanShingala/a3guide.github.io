Iotys Set
Experienced archers will understand that monsters that have ranged attacks can be quite dangerous. Therefore, those who prioritise speed over defence began to see the importance of ensuring more parts of their body are being protected. Iotys is made from a Phameino's leg. After the skin is removed, it will be soaked in a lethal poison for twelve days before it can be made into this armor
